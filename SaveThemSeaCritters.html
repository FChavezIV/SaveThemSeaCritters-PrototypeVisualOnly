How to Help

Learn about the marine species that are endangered and the reasons for their decline. Participate in beach clean-ups to remove trash from the shoreline. Support Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) Donate to or volunteer with organizations dedicated to marine conservation. Join projects that involve collecting data on marine life, which can help scientists in their research.

Safe Recreation Tips

Always keep a safe distance from marine animals, especially those that are endangered.

Avoid chasing, touching, or feeding marine life. This can stress them and alter their natural behaviors.

Follow designated paths to avoid trampling coral reefs, seagrass beds, and other fragile ecosystems.

Stay away from nesting sites, breeding grounds, and other sensitive habitats.

If you encounter injured or stranded marine animals, contact local authorities or marine rescue organizations. Do not attempt to help the animal yourself unless instructed to do so.
